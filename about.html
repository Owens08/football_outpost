About us

About us

Company History

FootBallOutPost Magazine was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate football aficionados with a shared vision of creating a publication that would capture the spirit and excitement of the world's most popular sport. United by their love for football and their desire to provide fans with a platform to immerse themselves in the game, they embarked on a remarkable journey.

In its infancy, FootBallOutPost started as a small publication, distributed locally to a modest readership. The early team worked tirelessly to bring readers engaging content, match reviews, player profiles, and insightful commentary. They established a strong foundation based on quality journalism and a commitment to delivering authentic and credible stories. With the increasing popularity of the sport and a growing appetite for football-related content, FootyFever Magazine saw an opportunity to expand its reach. The company invested in a digital presence, launching a dynamic website to complement the print edition. This move broadened their audience and allowed fans from across the globe to access their content.

As technology continued to advance, FootBallOutPost embraced the digital era wholeheartedly. The magazine transitioned to a fully digital format, embracing multimedia content, interactive features, and real-time updates. This transformation enabled the company to connect with an even broader international audience and engage fans in new and exciting ways.

Today, FootBallOutPost Magazine stands as a leading authority in football journalism, boasting a global readership spanning continents. The magazine is celebrated for its in-depth analysis, captivating storytelling, and commitment to sharing the diverse narratives that make football a universal language. Our dedication to fostering a vibrant community of football enthusiasts remains at the heart of our ethos. FootyFever continues to innovate and evolve, driven by the passion to provide football fans with an unparalleled reading experience. With every issue, we strive to bring you closer to the game you love, celebrating the triumphs, reliving the moments of magic, and connecting you with the heartbeat of football.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

At FootBallOutPost, our mission is to connect football lovers around the globe through our carefully curated magazine. We strive to deliver insightful analysis, captivating stories, exclusive interviews, and visually stunning features that capture the essence of the beautiful game.

At FootyFever Magazine, we envision a world where every football fan is united by the love for the game, connected through our stories, and inspired by the magic of football. We strive to be the beacon that illuminates the diverse tapestry of football, transcending borders and cultures. Our vision is to cultivate a global community of football enthusiasts who share in the passion, the drama, and the unifying power of the beautiful game.
